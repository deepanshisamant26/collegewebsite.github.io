Admission

Admissions to conventional courses are done on merit prepared based on qualifying exam (XII PCM% or PCB%/JEE' Rank, Valid CAT, MAT, XAT Score or GD/PI, UPTU-SEE/ UTU-SEE). The students should check eligibility criteria before applying. Based on the number of students and number of seats available in concerned departments, admissions are made strictly on merit basis as per the reservations rules of the university. The percentage of marks of the candidates shortlisted for admission corresponding to various categories as prescribed in reservation policy is displayed on the notice boards of the respective departments. Applications and challans form for Application fee maybe downloaded from the college website www.geu.ac.in or Online Application facility is also available on college website www.geu.ac.in. Candidates are required to submit duly filled-in application form along with photocopies of relevant documents, certificates, marks sheets etc. and proof of fee deposit at the college counters. The application fee through downloaded maybe deposited at various branches of Punjab National Bank (PNB) or through online transaction. Incomplete applications in any respect will not be entertained.
